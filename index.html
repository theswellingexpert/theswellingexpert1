Who We Serve

Conditions We Specialize In

Every condition that causes swelling affects your ability to live fully. This practice brings specialized, evidence-based care directly to you — meeting you in your environment, your routine, and your daily life.

01
Post-Cancer Lymphedema

Swelling resulting from cancer treatment, lymph node removal, or radiation — often affecting the arms or legs. Management focuses on reducing volume, preventing progression, and restoring function.

02
Chronic Lymphedema

Primary or secondary lymphedema requiring ongoing management. Complete Decongestive Therapy addresses both the acute and maintenance phases with a personalized long-term plan.

03
Venous Insufficiency

Chronic leg swelling, skin changes, and discomfort from impaired venous return. Compression therapy, movement retraining, and self-management strategies reduce symptoms and protect the skin.

04
Lipedema

A chronic condition causing abnormal fat and fluid accumulation — most often in the legs. Conservative management improves comfort, reduces heaviness, and supports daily function and quality of life.
