About the Program

Where Occupational Therapy Meets Lymphatic Care

The Back 2 Life Program is not just about managing swelling. It is about restoring your ability to do the things that matter most to you — returning to work, moving through your home with confidence, caring for your family, or simply feeling comfortable in your own body again.

As a Board Certified Occupational Therapist and CLWT, your specialist brings a uniquely holistic perspective. Every session addresses not just the physical symptoms of swelling, but how those symptoms affect your function, independence, and daily life.

This program complements, and does not replace, the medical management provided by your treating physician, hematologist, or vascular specialist.

Credentials in Practice

Board Certified Occupational Therapist (OT) and Certified Lymphedema and Wound Therapist (CLWT) with 15+ years of clinical experience across orthopedic rehabilitation, spinal cord injury, mental health, and lymphatic care.

Who This Program Serves

Post-surgical patients, individuals with venous insufficiency, lipedema, chronic swelling, and anyone whose daily function and quality of life is affected by swelling-related limitations.

How It's Delivered

All three sessions are delivered in-home throughout South Florida. No travel required on your part. Your specialist comes to you, works within your environment, and tailors every recommendation to your specific home and daily routine.

The Curriculum

Three Sessions. One Complete Program.

Each session builds on the last, moving you from understanding your body to managing it confidently and independently.

01
Session 1 · 60 Minutes · Assessment and Education
Know Your Body

This first session establishes the foundation for everything that follows. Before any intervention is introduced, your specialist invests time in truly understanding you — your health history, your daily life, what you do for work or at home, and what matters most to you. The goal is not just a clinical profile but a whole-person picture.

What's Covered
  • Occupational profile and whole-person health history
  • Limb assessment and baseline measurements
  • Lymphatic and venous system education
  • Compression contraindication review
  • Consent, goals, and program orientation
  • Physician coordination and documentation
02
Session 2 · 60 Minutes · Therapeutic Activity Training
Move With Confidence

Building skill and confidence in compression use and purposeful movement. This session bridges clinical interventions directly to your real life and meaningful daily occupations — because managing swelling is not just about treatment, it is about living fully.

What's Covered
  • Compression garment fitting, use, and care
  • Therapeutic movement and muscle pump techniques
  • Skin care and swelling self-assessment routine
  • Activity pacing and energy management
  • Environment and ergonomic modifications
  • Occupational performance goal review
03
Session 3 · 45 Minutes · Self-Management and Independence
Sustain and Prevent

Consolidating gains, establishing long-term prevention habits, and graduating with confidence. You leave this session with a clear self-management plan and the knowledge to recognize when to seek further care — because lasting results come from empowered clients, not dependent ones.

What's Covered
  • Personalized long-term prevention plan
  • Daily movement and compression routine
  • Red flag recognition and response guide
  • Follow-up pathway and referral coordination
  • Post-thrombotic syndrome awareness
  • Program graduation and self-management handoff
